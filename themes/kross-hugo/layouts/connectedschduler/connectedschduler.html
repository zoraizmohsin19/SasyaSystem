Do you have a need to schedule your pumps, venturi remotely? Do you want to know if the device got turned ON/OFF? Do you want to be free from uncertainty of Power Availability?
Sasya introduces Samaya Smart Scheduler – Samaya for complex scheduling requirements of devices (pumps, venturi, valves) for farms & Greenhouses. Samaya schedulers can be configured easily by experts remotely. Multiple devices can be scheduled in a given order and can be activated and tracked on Sasya IoT platform.



    Product Features

    Total 100 schedules can be set for a day. Basic Model 12 channel can be extended to 24 Channels (Relays) for Devices.
    Each Channel can be programmed independently, and has Manual ON/OFF.
    Incase of power failure remaining program will run when power resumes.
    Wifi connected , can be programmed , monitored and troubleshoot remotely. Programmed with Mobile or Computer.
