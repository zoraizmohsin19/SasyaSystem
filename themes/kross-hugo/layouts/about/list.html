Sasya means ‘Blue/Green’ or ‘Rain/Grain’
Sasya is a IOT technology company with focus on creating agriculture automation products for farmers and commercial growers


Vision & Mission Statement
We enable agriculture farms and greenhouses, helping farmers in automating and optimizing their farming activities, reducing costs and increasing yield

Our play
We provides IoT based Smart irrigation, Climate Control and Biofloc Culture Management products for agricultural fields, Green-houses and in-situ fisheries using Biofloc. We bring together sensors, communication gateways and the cloud technology along with Agri-knowledge for precision farming. 

Our Team
A group of technocrats with decades of experience in hardware design and fabrication, software system architecture, plant genetics, fertilizer and precision agriculture and above all passion for agriculture
